Change Request Gamification

In order to increase the quality of the Change Requests we discussed at the CAB meetings, I developed a scoring method. After implementing it in ServiceNow - Change Requests, I scored the change requests participating in the meetings for their quality. The points were added later and the points of the teams were declared at the end of each month. This situation started a sweet competition within the company and increased the change request quality. At the same time, that keeps low our IT Risk score, has also reduced the possibility of us having any incidents, bugs etc in the prod environment.
